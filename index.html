The Botany Roots

Botany Restaurant started as a small urban garden, driven by the belief that food should be as fresh as a forest morning. We stand out through our in-house hydroponic farm, ensuring every leaf is picked minutes before it reaches your plate. Our best-selling Midnight Orchid Tea and Emerald Mist Salad have become local legends for their unique colors and healing properties.
